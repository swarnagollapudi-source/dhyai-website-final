Textile

DHYAI works with cotton, muslin, silk and other natural fabrics.


The fabrics are chosen for how they drape, hold shadow, and soften with lived experience.


The current textile studies are not surface decoration. They are object families — bedsheets, bedcovers, dohars, table covers, and table linen sets — developed through repetition, spacing, weight, and use.

Cast Brass

Brass carries weight, warmth, and surface memory. It changes with handling and develops a living patina over time.


Brass pieces are sand-cast, lathe-finished, and satin-brushed.
