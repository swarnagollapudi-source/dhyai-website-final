Textiles

DHYAI works with pure cotton, muslin, and silk. The fabrics are chosen for how they drape, how they hold shadows, and how they soften with lived experience.

The current studies are families — bedsheets, bedcovers, dohars, table covers, table linen sets — developed through repetition, spacing, and use.

Cast Brass

The brass develops a living patina, where the surface deepens with use. And the weight of the metal is essential to its presence in a room.

Brass pieces are sand-cast, lathe-finished, and satin-brushed.
