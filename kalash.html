Kālāsh

कलश · a vessel

A form that has existed in this part of the world for as long as there has been a part of the world.

Cast in solid brass, satin-brushed, with a single engraving on the belly.

Made for puja, household ritual, and everyday presence.

Water in brass changes.
The vessel holds that change.

A full belly, a cast shoulder, a steadiness that requires no explanation.
