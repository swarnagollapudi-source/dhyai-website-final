Study One — Sthāna

स्थान · to be housed

A rose has been climbing the brick wall for some years.


The brick holds it, and the rose holds the brick.

Hand-block-printed in cotton, in a single field of greens, with six to seven rose medallions held within the brick lattice.


Formats: bedsheet, bedcover, dohar, table cover, table linen set.
