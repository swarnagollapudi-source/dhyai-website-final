Sthāna

A rose has been climbing the brick wall for some years. The brick holds it, and the rose holds the brick.

Sthāna is the cloth that comes from looking at this for long enough.

Hand-block-printed in cotton, in a single field of greens, with six to seven rose medallions held within the brick lattice.
