Study Two — Sthiti

स्थिति · the held moment

The same wall, on the day of a celebration.

The roses are still there. The brick is still there. Colour has entered the field because something in the house has shifted.

A design where colour and form have stopped moving against each other and simply arrived.

A grid that holds its ground.
A floral medallion that punctuates, not decorates.

The bedroom.
The dining table.
The room you return to without thinking.

Formats:
bedsheet / bedcover / dohar / table cover / table linen set
