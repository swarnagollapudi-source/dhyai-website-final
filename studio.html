The Studio


DHYAI is a material and surface design practice based in Bengaluru.


It works across objects, textiles, and material studies for homes, interiors, private events, gifting, and hospitality.


The name ध्यै is Sanskrit — to fix the mind upon.
A fold. A weight. A softened edge. A repeated touch.


Some objects mark a moment. Others remain after the moment has passed. Dhyai works with the latter.


Each piece begins with material. Weight, texture, density, and the way a surface interacts with light are treated as primary conditions.


The work is produced in small numbers, by hand, in workshops the studio has chosen to work with over time.
