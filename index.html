Objects that settle into life slowly are,

Time, embodied in material.
Objects meant to live with.

Object gravity

A piece belongs without justification. It carries its own weight in a room.

Objects through time

The object moves across homes and decades without becoming obsolete.

Object coherence

Material, use, ageing, and repetition resolve into quiet certainty.

“There is an experience. Objects happen inside it.”

Nothing seeks your attention.

And then you notice something — not because it announced itself, but because it exists and you arrived at it.

The Studio

DHYAI is a material and surface design practice based in Bengaluru. It works across objects, textiles, and material studies for homes, interiors, private events, gifting, and hospitality.

The name ध्यै is Sanskrit — to fix the mind upon.
A fold. A weight. A softened edge. A repeated touch.
Some objects mark a moment. Others remain after the moment has passed. Dhyai works with the latter.
