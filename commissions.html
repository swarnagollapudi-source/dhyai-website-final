Commissions


Commissions are how the studio enters another house.


The brief arrives, the work is drawn, and the pieces are made for a particular space, occasion, or gathering.


Weddings and private events

Return gifts, welcome objects, table programmes, ritual pieces — designed around a single occasion in a single house.

Corporate gifting

Considered gifting for senior teams, partners, and clients. Custom motifs, quiet packaging, and pieces made to stay beyond the event.

Hospitality interiors and private commissions

Surfaces drawn for a defined room or programme — a hotel suite, a restaurant table, a residential bedroom, a private cycle of gifting.
