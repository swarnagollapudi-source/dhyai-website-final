The Studio

Dhyai is a surface design and material practice based in Bengaluru.

It works across objects, textiles, and material studies for homes, interiors, private events, gifting, and hospitality.

The core philosophy of Dhyai is material expression of lived memory.

It is characterized by quiet intensity, sensory restraint, and living lines rather than rigid perfection.

The studio focuses on the intersection of technical product engineering, architectural structure, surface rhythm, and tactile permanence.

Objects are not viewed as isolated pieces, but as part of a room that gathers around them.

The work is produced in small numbers, by hand, in workshops the studio has chosen to work with over time.

Nothing is hurried.
